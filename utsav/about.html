About LEO Utsav

Leo Utsav, a blend of extra-curricular activities, is conducted for the students of all campuses of Anna University. Primarily conducted to bring the talents of the first years to the limelight, Leo Utsav aims at contributing towards the society. The main aim of this year’s event is to providing a helping hand to ‘VINMEENGAL’, an organization that aims at the educational progress of the underprivileged children, and to make our friends realize the needs of children to study. The sponsorship and registration funds raised will be contributed for the education and personality development of downtrodden children.
