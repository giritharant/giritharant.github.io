LEO CLUB OF CEG

ordinary people amazing things

     LEO stands for ‘Leadership Experience Opportunity’. True to the acronym, we have always been proving our mettle in honing the skills of the Leos by exposing ourselves to plethora of opportunities, pertaining to social service, personality development and the like. We are of the notion that
‘Service is not to be spoken about without involvement; instead, it can be experienced only by immersing oneself through body and soul into helping others’.

LEADERSHIP:
     Leadership is not just going in front of a pack or crew. It’s about getting prepared to confront challenges, hurry through hardships and above all, instil an optimistic thought process in oneself and one’s fellow mates. The primary goal of the Leo movement is to bring about a drastic within the insights of the Leos to transform them into human beings brimming with positive energy, confident intent and unbridled energy.

EXPERIENCE:
     Every baby learns to walk only after toddling; every renowned personality starts as an amateur; every achievement starts with the first step – the experience. And experience, in turn, comes from social interactions, interpersonal skills, managerial abilities and the presence of mind to take care of things when something goes wrong or out of place. This is what Leoism is all about.

OPPORTUNITY:
     : Any Body Can Develop but one needs the platform to showcase the skillset to start the progress. The Leo Club provides the platform for adequate opportunities to develop one’s self, while developing the society through gradual steps that bring about unthinkable changes. Talented people do exist, but only those who get opportune moments to exhibit their talents become experts..
