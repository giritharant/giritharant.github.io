     The Leo Club of CEG, in collaboration with the Manidhaneyam IAS Academy, one of the most sought after coaching centre in Tamil Nadu, brings coaching classes right at our campus. Be it aptitude, logical reasoning, economics, polity or whatsoever, these classes are sure to provide you the right frame in every respect. So, get geared up to stamp your first step towards Civil Services.

  • Will I be able to attend these classes amidst college schedule?
    The classes will take place from 5 P. M. to 7 P. M., which would have no effect on the college timings.

    No, it is absolutely free of cost.
