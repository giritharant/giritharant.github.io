Utsav literally means ‘a function to celebrate boundlessly’.
How about a kind of funny cultural festival organized by the club that lays emphasis on social service?
Service can be effective only when lighter moments of enjoyment are inhaled.
Truggle to study, due to monetary and societal reasons.

Conducted especially for the non – leos within the college by the Leo Club of CEG, this two – day cultural conglomerate comprises of a plethora of events like Quizzes, Puzzles, Oratorical and Writing Contests to stimulate the unearthed talents of the students.The entry fee (don’t sweat it out! Just a two – digit amount) collected for the events would duly be donated to the needy based on the situation, after giving away the prizes to the winners.

  • Who can participate in Leo Utsav?
    Any student of CEG or ACT, who is not a member of the Leo Club are allowed to participate.
  • Will a non – CEGian be allowed to take part?
    No. The events are meant strictly for the people from the Campus.
  • How will I find Leo Utsav different from the other fun fests?
    The events conducted focus not only in providing the fun quotient but also enhancing the educational outlook of the participants. In the Leo Utsav – 2015, we organized a guest lecture by Manhattan Review to provide insights into the nuances to crack competitive examinations like GRE, GMAT, TOEFL, IELTS, etc.
