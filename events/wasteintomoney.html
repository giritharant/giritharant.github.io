Wish to lend a helping hand in our activities?
Hello there, we welcome you with pleasure to be a part of our community service.
All you need is a bit of charitable mind.


     Just collect all the old newspapers, magazines and used notebooks in your home and give it to us. We promise that the fund raised through this will be used only for a cause.

  • Where can I give the materials mentioned?
    We will be coming periodically to different areas in and around the campus like Kotturpuram and Adyar to collect the papers. So, never worry!
  • I am not a resident of the areas mentioned above. How do I contribute?
    We are planning to expand our radius to more areas. Further details will be made available soon.
  • How do I know you are from Leo Club of CEG?
    All members of the club have unique identification cards, which will be shown at the time of our arrival.
  • Can money and clothes be given as contribution?
    As of now, we collect only papers. So, clothes and money will NOT BE ENTERTAINED.
